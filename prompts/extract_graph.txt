
-Goal-
Given an aviation accident report or related text, identify all entities relevant to the "Human-Machine-Environment-Management" (人-机-环-管) framework and all causal/descriptive relationships among them. The goal is to construct a knowledge graph that enables root-cause analysis.

-Entity Types-
- person (人): Pilots, crew members, air traffic controllers, maintenance personnel, etc.
- equipment (机/设备): Aircraft, engines, avionics, ground equipment, tools, etc.
- environment (环/环境): Weather conditions, airport conditions, terrain, lighting, etc.
- organization (管/组织/管理): Airlines, regulatory bodies, maintenance organizations, management policies, etc.
- procedure (程序): Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs), manuals, checklists, etc.
- event (事件): Specific occurrences during the accident sequence (e.g., engine flameout, hard landing).
- weather (天气): Specific meteorological factors.
- violation (违规操作): Non-compliance with regulations or procedures.

-Relationship Types-
- leads to / causes (导致): One event or factor directly or indirectly resulting in another.
- limited by (受限于): An entity's performance or state is constrained by another factor.
- violates (违反): A person or action not following a procedure or regulation.
- not executed (未执行): A required procedure or action that was omitted.
- belongs to (属于): An entity is part of an organization or category.

-Steps-
1. Identify all entities. For each identified entity, extract the following information:
- entity_name: Name of the entity, capitalized
- entity_type: One of the types listed above: [{entity_types}]
- entity_description: Detailed description of the entity's role, state, or attributes in the context of the accident.
Format each entity as ("entity"<|><entity_name><|><entity_type><|><entity_description>)
 
2. Identify all pairs of (source_entity, target_entity) that have a clear causal, constraint, or structural relationship.
For each pair of related entities, extract:
- source_entity: name of the source entity
- target_entity: name of the target entity
- relationship_description: explanation of the relationship (e.g., "The pilot failed to monitor the altitude, leading to the crash")
- relationship_strength: a numeric score (1-10) indicating the significance of this link in the accident chain.
 Format each relationship as ("relationship"<|><source_entity><|><target_entity><|><relationship_description><|><relationship_strength>)
 
3. Return output in English (or the language of the source text if preferred, but usually English for consistency in KG) as a single list. Use **##** as the list delimiter.
 
4. When finished, output <|COMPLETE|>
